Which Ski Pass Should You Get?

The Epic Pass, IKON Pass and Mountain Collective are three of the most popular ski passes on the market. While all three passes offer access to a number of different resorts, which one is the best value?.

$1450AUD

With the Epic Pass, you get unlimited access to any Vail Resorts resort. The pass also gives you discounts on lodging at all of these resorts. You also receive days at several reciprocal resorts.

$1700AUD

The IKON Pass is designed for those who want to explore a variety of different destinations over the course of a season. It gives the holder unlimited access many resorts in North America as well as several different international destinations.

$980AUD

The Mountain Collective Pass is best for those who want to go big on their ski trips without breaking the bank. It grants you unlimited access to some of the biggest and most popular resorts around the world. This includes a total of 26 days of skiing.
